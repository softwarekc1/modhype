Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install these mods?
Each mod comes with detailed installation instructions. Typically you'll need to copy files to your game's installation directory. We provide step-by-step guides for each mod and video tutorials for popular games.
Are these mods free to use?
Yes, all mods on MobVex are completely free. We do require a quick verification to access downloads, which helps support our platform and keeps the mods free for everyone.
Will these mods get me banned?
While we test all mods extensively, we can't guarantee they're undetectable. We recommend using mods in single-player modes only and checking each mod's details for specific warnings. Many mods are marked with their online compatibility status.
How often are new mods added?
Our library is updated daily with new content. We have a team of modders constantly creating and testing new modifications. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified about major new releases.
Can I request specific mods?
Absolutely! We welcome mod requests from our community. While we can't fulfill every request, popular suggestions get prioritized. Contact us with your mod ideas and we'll do our best to make them happen.
